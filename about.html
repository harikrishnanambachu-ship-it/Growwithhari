About Us

We Don't Advise From the Sidelines. We Build and Raise Alongside You.

Our Story

Grow With Hari was founded on a simple frustration: founders were getting fundraising advice from people who had never built anything, and build advice from people who didn't understand what investors actually look for.

We closed that gap by doing both ourselves first.

Harikrishnan Kalarikkal Joshi, founder of Grow With Hari, built BrainCode — a full Flutter and Supabase ed-tech platform with three integrated applications and live video infrastructure — solo, as a non-technical founder, using AI as the primary execution partner. That same approach now powers how we help other founders build.

On the funding side, we've prepared pitch decks, financial models, due diligence reports, and government grant documentation that have been used in real investor and lender conversations — not templates, built case by case.

We work in one mode: milestone-based, hands-on, no black box. You know what's being built, why, and what it costs to get there.

Harikrishnan Kalarikkal Joshi
Founder, Grow With Hari
