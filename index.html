The Program

8 Weeks to Investor Ready

A structured week-by-week program that takes you from idea or existing product to fully investor-ready — with documents, training, expert mentorship and revisions.

Week 1
Strategic Foundation & Planning
"Before we build anything — we align everything."
We deeply understand your startup — vision, market, product, and competition. This week builds the complete strategic foundation that every document and pitch will be built on.
Deliverables
Vision & Mission document
SWOT analysis report
Go-to-Market (GTM) strategy
Product growth strategy
Competitor landscape analysis
Target audience & market sizing
Investors don't just fund products — they fund founders who deeply understand their market.
Week 2
Current Financial Analysis
"Know your numbers before an investor questions them."
We analyse your current financial position honestly and clearly — presenting your financials in a way investors understand and trust.
Deliverables
Current P&L statement
Balance sheet preparation
Cash flow statement
Burn rate & runway analysis
Fund requirement analysis
Investor-ready financial summary
Most startups lose investor confidence because their financials are messy or unclear.
Week 3
10-Year Financial Projections
"Show investors where you're going — not just where you are."
We build a comprehensive 10-year financial model based on real assumptions — showing investors the size of the opportunity and the return they can expect.
Deliverables
10-year revenue model with assumptions
10-year P&L, balance sheet & cash flow
Company valuation (pre & post money)
Equity dilution & cap table
Phase-wise funding requirement plan
KPI & KRA framework
Investors invest in the future — not the present. A strong 10-year model builds immediate credibility.
Week 4
★ Two Decks Delivered
Pitch Deck Creation
"Two decks. One for depth. One for impact."
We create two powerful pitch decks — a detailed 60-slide deck for depth, and a sharp 20-25 slide investor deck designed to win the room.
Deliverables
60-slide detailed pitch deck
20-25 slide investor pitch deck
Executive summary document
Teaser / introductory deck
Data room documentation checklist
Most consultants give you one deck. We give you two — because different conversations need different depth.
Week 5
Pitch Training
"A great deck means nothing if you can't deliver it."
We train you to present with clarity, confidence and conviction — handling tough questions, structuring your narrative, and controlling the room.
What We Cover
Pitch delivery & storytelling techniques
Handling tough investor questions
Body language & confidence building
Deck walkthrough & timing practice
Q&A simulation sessions
Documents get you through the door. Your pitch delivery gets you the cheque.
Week 6
Expert Session
Mock Pitch with Experts
"Pitch to real experts before you pitch to real investors."
You deliver your full pitch to a panel of experienced founders and industry mentors who evaluate you exactly like real investors would.
What Happens
Full mock pitch with expert panel
Real-time feedback on delivery & content
Detailed written feedback report
Specific revision plan for next rounds
Nothing prepares you better than pitching to people who've been on both sides of the table.
Week 7
5 Rounds of Revision
Revision & Mentorship with Hari
"Every round makes you sharper. Every revision makes you stronger."
Based on expert feedback we refine your pitch, update your documents, and practice again — up to 5 rounds of revision until your pitch is truly ready.
What Happens
Up to 5 rounds of pitch revision
Document updates after each round
1-on-1 mentorship sessions with Hari
Final pitch sign-off
We don't stop until your pitch is genuinely ready to win funding.
Week 8
Final Week
Legal Documentation & Final Closing
"Get legally ready to close your first round."
We prepare all the legal documents you need to close your funding round — so when an investor says yes, you're ready to move fast.
Deliverables
Investor term sheet template
NDA templates for investors
Investment agreement framework
Cap table management templates
Due diligence preparation checklist
Complete data room setup
When an investor says yes — you need to move fast. Week 8 makes sure you're ready.
